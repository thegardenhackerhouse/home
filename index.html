Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why is The Garden only open to women?

    For our pilot experience, we want to bring together a community of females in the fields of tech & science where female representation in generally low compared to males. Most co-living experiences are dominated by male representation, so our goal is to build a hacker house that specifically targets women and encourages them to build communities together. We want to create a unique experience that allows women to feel vulnerable & open to connect with others who have similar lived experiences, that can be lost when you mix in both men & women. In the future, as The Garden community grows, we may open up co-ed options if there is sufficient interest. If you are a male and are extremely interested in participating, please reach out to one of our organizers to discuss co-ed possibilities.

  • Is The Garden a one-time event?

    Absolutely not! The Daly City 2021 experience is our pilot house but certainly not our last. Long term, we hope to build The Garden into a lasting network of friends, coworkers, and mentors. We hope to not only give our women experiential but also financial support to fund their projects and ideas. This is the beginning of something beautiful, and you have the opportunity to become a part!

  • Why should I be a part of this experience?

    The Garden is the first of its kind: a female-focused experience that promotes female empowerment in technology, business, and outreach. The pilot house is the beginning of a lasting community that will cultivate the next generation of strong female leadership. We strive to establish a network of women of all different backgrounds, ethnicities, educational levels, and interests who share our common goals.

  • What are the living arrangements?

    Each member will rent out a room in the house for the two month experience with shared common spaces.

  • What kind of activities can I expect at The Garden?

    1. Recreational: Hiking, Kayaking, Beach Trips, City Adventures, etc.
    2. Educational: Demos and Pitch Nights, Hackathons, Book Clubs, Learning Groups
    3. Community-Building: Networking, Mentorship, Guest Speakers, and Friendships!
  • Who can apply?

    Young women with an entrepraneurial drive of ALL backgrounds between the ages of 18-25 are welcome to apply!

  • How do I apply?

    Click the apply now button to fill out our applicant interest survey, and our organizers will reach out to follow up!

  • When are applications due?

    We'd like to have our cohort membership will be finalized by the end of May.
